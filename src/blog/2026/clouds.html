About a month ago my wife and I made our usual trek to see her parents. We started our drive later than usual so we were still on the road early evening (~5pm). The clouds in the sky were dramatic, and I wanted a picture. But I wanted something a little different.

I've been thinking about trying to take pictures from different perspectives lately, so I opened my camera app and pointed it straight up, snapping pictures through the car windshield. The results pre-edits were stunning but I wanted to punch them up a little more in Lightroom.
