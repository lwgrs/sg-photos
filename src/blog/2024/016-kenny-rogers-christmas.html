Winter and Christmas provide an excuse for people to decorate their house with inflatables and lighting - not dissimilar to Halloween. In my neighbourhood there is one particular house that caught my eye, but not because it's a shining example of creativity. It's very simple: the house is red, like really red. It really reminds me of the Seinfeld episode about a Kenny Rogers Chicken opening across from Kramer's apartment and the bright red lighting.
